Florian Clemente - artwork

My passion for painting started while living in the South of France, where I got inspired by encountering many of the original landscapes eternalized by Vincent Van Gogh. Initially, I taught myself to paint by trying to replicate some of Vincent's works or borrowing some of his ideas and combining them with mine. More recently, I have also gained experience in other painting styles and I am curious to see where this will lead me to...
